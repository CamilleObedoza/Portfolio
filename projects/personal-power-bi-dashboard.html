Case Study

Personal Power BI Dashboard

A dashboard I built for myself to actually understand my own spending, since bank statements alone weren't telling me much — and a good excuse to practice real data modeling.

Power BI DAX Power Query

Overview

My spending was scattered across bank exports and spreadsheets, which made it hard to spot trends or catch overspending early. I cleaned and standardized the transaction exports, built a proper data model with a date table and category dimension, then layered DAX measures on top for totals, averages, and month-over-month comparisons.

Once things were categorized correctly, it turned out a handful of small recurring charges were adding up to more than any single big purchase — something the raw bank export view never made obvious.
