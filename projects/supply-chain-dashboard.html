Case Study

Supply Chain Dashboard

A working dashboard for tracking inventory and order status, built so operations staff could check one screen instead of several spreadsheets every morning.

Power BI DAX SQL

Overview

Operations staff had to check multiple spreadsheets just to answer basic questions — current stock levels, orders at risk of delay, which SKUs were trending toward a stockout. I consolidated inventory, order, and fulfillment data into one model, then worked with operations staff to define what "at risk" actually meant before building any visuals.

The risk-flagging logic ended up surfacing that a specific group of fast-moving SKUs consistently ran close to stockout right before restock day — meaning their reorder point had been set too low for actual demand.
