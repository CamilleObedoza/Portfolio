About

I recently graduated from Brigham Young University–Hawaii with a Bachelor of Science in Business Management and Applied Mathematics. My academic background gave me a strong foundation in analytical thinking, quantitative problem-solving, and business strategy, which naturally led me to pursue data analytics, business intelligence, and supply chain management.

Throughout my academic journey, internship, and professional experiences, I worked on projects involving data analytics, logistics, supply chain operations, and process improvement. I developed interactive dashboards using Power BI and Tableau, analyzed data with SQL and Excel, created global logistics documentation, and collaborated on supply chain initiatives focused on improving operational efficiency and supporting data-driven decision-making. I enjoy transforming complex data into meaningful insights that help organizations make informed business decisions.

Beyond analytics, I value organization, process improvement, and clear communication. Whether I'm building dashboards, documenting workflows, or analyzing operational data, I strive to create solutions that are practical, easy to understand, and designed to make an impact.

I'm currently seeking opportunities as a Data Analyst, Business Intelligence Analyst, Supply Chain Analyst, Logistics Analyst, or Operations Analyst, where I can continue learning, solve meaningful business challenges, and help organizations turn data into actionable insights.
